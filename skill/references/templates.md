# Templates

Adapt these templates to your vault. Keep the sections that force useful thinking: mechanism, limits, links, and sources.

## Concept

```markdown
---
created: YYYY-MM-DD
updated: YYYY-MM-DD
type: concept
status: draft
map: "[[Parent map]]"
tags: [domain, topic]
---

# Title

## Summary
Three to four sentences: what the concept is, why it matters, and the central tension.

## Definition and reformulation
- Canonical definition, if useful
- Personal reformulation
- Origin or trigger
- Real examples
- What this is not

## Intellectual territory

### What supports it
Evidence, cases, patterns, or practical observations.

### Implicit assumptions
What the idea takes for granted.

### Limits and counterexamples
Where it breaks, misleads, or needs context.

### Opposing or complementary models
Adjacent models and when they work better.

## Connections
- [[Existing note]] - reason for the connection

## Applications
- Practical use cases

## Production angles
- **Angle 1**: a defendable claim or article angle

## Sources
- [[Source file]] - distilled on YYYY-MM-DD
```
